SOCIAL COMMITTEE

SkillsUSA is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization working to foster self-sustaining, robust, local entrepreneurial ecosystems in underserved communities across the United States. We are almost 100% dependent on the support of donors like you so that we can offer our programs and services at no cost to the entrepreneurs we serve.

SkillsUSA programs include intensive business training, a team of 3 dedicated mentors per entrepreneur as well as an extended professional network. This powerful community of funders, volunteers and staff enables every entrepreneur to feel more confident, connected and informed as he/she works to start or grow their small business.
